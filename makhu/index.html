About • Linatey

Linatey Khumalo started hair braiding and plaiting from a tender age of 10. After graduating from a beauty school in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, she spent 3 years as a hairstylist in Bulawayo and Johannesburg. Since 2010, she has been an independent stylist at salons in Hillbrow, a thriving market district in the heart of Johannesburg.
